Hello!

My name is Shirin Alimirzaei, and I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Lethbridge.

My research lies in algebraic graph theory, with a particular focus on automorphisms of Cayley graphs, including colour-preserving and colour-permuting automorphisms.

I am also interested in teaching mathematics and supporting students through tutorials and problem-solving.
